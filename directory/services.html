Services

Reverse Engineering

Replacement or backup parts to keep your productions running full speed ahead

If you are in need of replacement parts or backup parts, Eton Controls has just the solution for you. Over the last 20 years of reverse engineering experience, we’ve seen just about everything, and we are well prepared to tackle any replacement need your company may have.

Any part is something that we can reproduce using the latest technology and most advanced equipment on the market. Our reverse engineering service is the best in the business -- whether your parts are completely new on the market, worn down with the effects of time and wear and tear, or even if they’ve now been rendered obsolete, we can reverse engineer any trim part to provide you with the replacement you need.

We employ the use of CMM measurement and 3-D CAD equipment to ensure a precise reproduction alongside our decades of manufacturing expertise. No matter the challenge at hand, no matter the tightness of the timeline, and no matter the type of part you present to us, Eton Controls excels at providing the ideal solution with a rapid turnaround time to keep your company’s production flowing.

Turbine Parts

We are well-equipped to manufacture all types of turbine parts

We are endlessly driven to maintaining a huge inventory of OEM turbine parts, and we have the ability to both repair and manufacture turbine parts of just about any kind. Our commitment to deploying the latest technological advances coupled with our unparalleled stock of parts enables us to address turbine parts of all kinds, ranging from Toshiba to GE to Alstom and many more. Our large stock enables us to ship out our parts quickly, often times even within the same day.

Our capabilities cover a broad span of turbine parts and valves, including (but certainly not limited to):

  • Bushings
  • Keeper rings
  • Pressure-seal heads
  • Valve stems
  • Bellows

And much more! We can even upgrade existing materials in order to extend the life of your machinery parts. For any turbine valve repair or manufacturing needs, get in touch with us to discuss the details.
