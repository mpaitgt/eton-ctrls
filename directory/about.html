About Us

Eton Controls is the parts repair service you can trust.

With over 20 years of experience repairing and reverse engineering machine parts, the experts at Eton Controls bring a degree of know-how and attention to detail that you won’t find anywhere else.

Our extensive knowledge of machinery and machine parts paired with our commitment to high-quality customer service enables us to provide a truly exceptional experience for our customers. No matter the challenge at hand, no matter how common or rare the issue we’re presented, we’re ready to tackle any request that comes our way and come up with both time-tested and innovative solutions to yield excellent results.

We offer a broad variety of services to help us meet the needs of our customers. From repairs and reverse engineering to precision machining to valve work and beyond, we are knowledgeable and well-equipped to address our customers’ concerns.

Why us?

What sets us apart is our ability to meet and anticipate our customer’s needs at a speed that won’t keep you waiting.

We take care to keep a well-stocked warehouse of machine parts and original OEM turbine parts, which contributes to our ability to provide a fast turnaround time by eliminating the prospect of having to wait for parts to ship to us. Plus, our reverse engineering service lends us the ability to help our customers keep their operations running without a hitch by supplying backup parts for crucial equipment and machines.

Above all else, customer satisfaction is our number one priority. We work tirelessly to meet and exceed our customers’ expectations in order to provide a service that can’t be beat. We strive to continually improve the services that we provide in order to remain the trusted repair and reverse engineering service in the industry.
